Technical Support

What does my computer need to use the Form Filler?

Your computer needs to meet these minimum requirements:

  • Internet browser with 128-bit encryption support
  • Adobe Acrobat Reader version 7.0 or higher
  • JavaScript enabled in your browser
  • Recommended: Either an ink-jet or laser printer

The website displays blank or incomplete forms. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  2. Ensure JavaScript is enabled in your browser
  3. Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge)
  4. Check if you meet the minimum system requirements
  5. If issues persist, try restarting your computer

Why do I receive an "unknown file type" message when trying to open my form?

This typically occurs when Adobe Reader isn't properly installed. To resolve:

  1. Verify Adobe Reader is installed on your computer
  2. If not installed, download the latest version from Adobe's website
  3. If installed, try removing and reinstalling Adobe Reader
  4. Ensure your browser is set to open PDF files with Adobe Reader

My form doesn't print properly. How can I fix this?

To ensure proper printing:

  • Use the latest version of Adobe Reader
  • Set your printer settings to "Actual Size" (not "Fit to Page")
  • Ensure you have enough ink/toner
  • Try printing as a PDF first, then print the PDF
  • Use the print preview to check formatting before printing

Process & Requirements

How long does it take to complete a form?

Most forms take 15-30 minutes to complete, depending on:

  • The complexity of your situation
  • Whether you have all required information ready
  • The type of form you're filling out

You can save your progress and return later if needed.

What information do I need to have ready?

Required information varies by form type but generally includes:

  • Personal identification information
  • Relevant dates and locations
  • Case numbers (if applicable)
  • Financial information (for certain forms)
  • Supporting documentation

Can I save my progress and complete the form later?

Yes, you can save your progress at any time. To do this:

  1. Click the "Save Progress" button
  2. Log into your account when you return
  3. Find your saved form under "My Forms"
  4. Continue where you left off

Your progress is automatically saved every few minutes as well.

Can I edit a form after printing/downloading it?

Once you print or download a form as a PDF, you cannot edit it directly on our website. However, you may be able to use a PDF editor to make changes to the downloaded file. For best results, make all necessary edits before downloading or printing your form.

Legal & Compliance

Is FormWiz a law firm?

No, FormWiz is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. We are a legal document preparation service that:

  • Helps you complete necessary legal forms
  • Provides tools to organize your information
  • Offers guidance on form requirements
  • Recommends consulting with an attorney for legal advice

Are my completed forms legally valid?

Yes, forms completed through FormWiz are legally valid when:

  • All required information is provided accurately
  • Forms are properly signed and dated
  • Any necessary notarization is completed
  • Forms are filed with the appropriate authorities

However, we recommend reviewing important documents with a legal professional before filing.

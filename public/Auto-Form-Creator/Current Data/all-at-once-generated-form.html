Contributing Agency Information

What is the contributing agency's ORI code?

Enter the ORI (Originating Agency Identifier) code assigned to the contributing agency by the DOJ. This identifies the agency submitting the live scan — not your personal information.

What is the contributing agency's authorized applicant type?

Enter the authorized applicant type code for this submission, as assigned or required by the DOJ for your agency's live scan category.

What is the contributing agency's type of license/certification/permit?

Enter the type of license, certification, permit, or working title (maximum 30 characters). Use the exact title assigned by the DOJ if applicable.

What is the name of the agency authorized to receive criminal record information?

Enter the contributing agency's agency authorized to receive criminal record information exactly as it should appear in the "Contributing Agency Information" section on the PDF. This is about the contributing agency, not about you personally.

What is the contributing agency's mail code?

Enter the five-digit mail code assigned to the contributing agency by the Department of Justice. This is in the Contributing Agency Information section — not your personal address.

What is the contributing agency's address?

This asks for the contributing agency's street address or P.O. Box — not your personal home address. Find it in the "Contributing Agency Information" section on the PDF.

Who is the agency contact person?

Enter the contributing agency's contact name exactly as it should appear in the "Contributing Agency Information" section on the PDF. This is about the contributing agency, not about you personally.

What is the contributing agency's contact telephone number?

Enter the contact telephone number for the contributing agency — the person who can be reached about this submission.

What is the contributing agency's billing number?

Enter the contributing agency's billing number exactly as it should appear in the "Contributing Agency Information" section on the PDF. This is about the contributing agency, not about you personally.

Applicant Information

What is your name as shown on your tax return?

Enter the name exactly as it appears on your income tax return. For individuals, use your legal name; for entities, use the registered business name.

What is your business name or disregarded entity name, if different from above?

Enter your business name or disregarded entity name if it differs from the name on line 1. Leave blank if the same.

Please select your federal tax classification

Select the option or options that best apply to your situation.

What is the tax classification of the LLC owner?

If you checked LLC above, enter C for corporation, S for S corporation, or P for partnership — the tax classification of the LLC owner shown on the W-9.

What is your other tax classification?

Only complete this if you selected "Other" for federal tax classification. Enter the classification exactly as it should appear on the form.

Please confirm: I have foreign partners, owners, or beneficiaries

Check this box if you are a partnership, trust, or estate with foreign partners, owners, or beneficiaries, and you are providing this form to a partnership, trust, or estate in which you have an ownership interest.



Applicant Information

What is your address?

Enter your home street address or P.O. Box as the applicant. This is your personal residence — not the agency or employer address.

What is the requester's name and address (optional)?

Enter your home street address or P.O. Box as the applicant. This is your personal residence — not the agency or employer address.

What is your account number(s) for this request (optional)?

Enter your account number(s) for this request (optional) exactly as it should appear on the completed form in the Applicant Information section.



Applicant Information

What is your taxpayer identification number?

Enter your taxpayer identification number (SSN for individuals, EIN for businesses) exactly as it should appear on the W-9.

What is your employer identification number?

Enter the employer identification number (EIN) for the business entity, if applicable.

What name and number should you give the requester for EIN purposes?

Enter your name and number to give the requester exactly as it should appear on the completed form in the Applicant Information section.

What name should be used for SSN purposes?

Enter your name associated with this social security number exactly as it should appear on the completed form in the Applicant Information section.

What is your additional name or DBA (if applicable)?

Enter your additional name or dba (if applicable) exactly as it should appear on the completed form in the Applicant Information section.

What is your exempt payee code?

Enter an exempt payee code only if you are exempt from backup withholding under IRS rules. Most individuals leave this blank.

What is your FATCA reporting code?

Enter a FATCA reporting code only if you are exempt from FATCA reporting. Most individuals leave this blank.

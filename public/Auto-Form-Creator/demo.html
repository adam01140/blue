Step 2 — Unlock XFA Form

Upload an XFA PDF and our tool automatically strips the XFA layer to reveal the underlying AcroForm fields — the same result as printing through Adobe PDF, but done instantly on the server. Duplicate field names (common on radio groups) are split into uniquely named checkboxes so each widget can be filled independently.

Step 3 — Extract Form Fields

The unlocked PDF is analyzed to find every text field and checkbox, mapped to their positions in the layout. Text fields appear as [[field]] and checkboxes as {{field}}. Each widget gets its own entry — duplicate names are resolved during unlock.

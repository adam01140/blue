Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to provide email and personal information?

We use your account information solely to fill out your forms accurately and securely. Your privacy is our top priority, and your data is never shared or sold. This information is required by courts and agencies to process your paperwork.

What if I have a lawyer already?

If you already have a lawyer, you can still use Form-Star to prepare your paperwork. Simply review your completed forms with your attorney before filing. Form-Star does not provide legal advice or representation.
